“About”

Hi, I’m Luyao 🦠🌍🍄🌱

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Botany, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on understanding how microbial communities regulate ecosystem processes under global change. I am broadly interested in microbial ecology, community ecology, and global change ecology, with a particular emphasis on biogeochemical processes and their underlying drivers and consequences. I am committed to open and reproducible science, as well as effective scientific communication.

🎯 Research Interests

  • 🦠🌍 Microbial ecology & biogeochemistry
    Microbial community assembly and its implications for soil biogeochemical processes under global change

  • 🧬💻 Integrative multi-omics & bioinformatics
    Applying multi-omics approaches and computational tools to unravel microbial ecological mechanisms
